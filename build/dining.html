Breakfast

A cheerful start to your morning

Locally sourced and lovingly prepared every morning, breakfast at Silver Strand Guesthouse is a morning treat to savour and enjoy at leisure.

Served at communal tables inside and in the glass conservatory, tables are laden with freshly baked breads,
homemade preserves, not to mention famous and moreish cranberry and orange nut loaf.

Options include fresh berries, poached pears in vanilla syrup,
homemade granola and muesli and artisan yoghurt and cream.

Cooked to order dishes include Kippers, Eggs Benedict, mushroom frittata, scrambled egg with smoked salmon and light and airy omelettes with a choice of delicious fillings.
