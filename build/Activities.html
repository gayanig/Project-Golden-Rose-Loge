Site seeing

You can take a boat trip to the Fastnet Rock Lighthouse – also known as the teardrop of Ireland,
because it was the last part of Ireland that Irish emigrants saw as they sailed to America.Duration: approx 2hrs.
Sherkin has three beautiful sandy beaches – Silver Strand, Cow Strand, and Trabawn – which are ideal for secluded swimming and coastal walks.
Wander along the road past banks of red fuschia, bright orange mombrisha and rocky fields hemmed in by dry stone walls.
Explore the old lighthouse and the ruined Franciscan Abbey by the pier.
The island is also well known for its magical sunsets.
