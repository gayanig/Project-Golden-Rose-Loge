Sherkin Island lies in Roaringwater Bay, West Cork, in the far south-west of Ireland. Just three miles long and one and a half miles wide, Sherkin has a unique beauty and variety of landscape,
which is both tranquil and breathtaking.

There is a regular ferry service between the village of Baltimore and Sherkin Island,
with a journey time of just ten minutes – making Sherkin one of Ireland’s most accessible offshore islands.
